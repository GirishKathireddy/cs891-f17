This class will provide an in-depth exploration of advanced research
issues surrounding web archiving, with emphasis on past, current,
and future work of the ODU Web Science and Digital Libraries (WS-DL)
resarch group.  Topics will include: web archiving basics, the W3C
Web Architecture, a survey of open issues in web archiving, research
methodology, and a review of tools and proficiencies necessary for
success with the WS-DL group.  Students must be comfortable with
self-directed learning appropriate for an advanced graduate class.
